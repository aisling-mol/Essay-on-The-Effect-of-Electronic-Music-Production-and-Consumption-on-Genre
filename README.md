# Essay-on-The-Effect-of-Electronic-Music-Production-and-Consumption-on-Genre
As part of a college project for my Computer Music module, I was given a prompt to discuss and research the effect of electronic music production and consumption on genre. I'm proud of how it came out and decided to upload my final submission here too :)

## The full prompt was as follows;

"The advent of music streaming platforms, the demise of the album format and the rise of bedroom producers with laptops has had a profound effect on the way we produce and consume music. Most notably, the once fundamental concept of genre has become less defined and rigid. For example, Danger Mouse combined the hip-hop of Jay-Z and classic rock the of Beatles on his infamous mashup The Grey Album, while the late Polish modernist Krzysztof Penderecki and Warp Records stalwart Aphex Twin collaborated effortlessly on their "Remote Orchestra". "
